# Venue Lost Item Recovery Kit

Organize fast, safe outreach to cafes, gyms, taxis, hotels, and venues after a lost item, with contact tracking and ownership verification templates.

## What it does
- Quickly captures item facts and safe identifiers without exposing sensitive details
- Ranks likely locations and generates venue-appropriate message templates
- Provides a contact tracker log for following up with multiple locations
- Plans safe ownership verification and pickup at public, staffed locations

## Example scenarios

**1. Lost jacket at a hotel with no response**
> 👤 "I left a black leather jacket at a Marriott in Chicago room 412 on 6/10. The hotel hasn't responded to my call."
> 🤖 Generates a formal lost-item claim email with room number, date, item description, and a request for the hotel's lost-and-found log check. Includes a photo attachment placeholder.

**2. Follow-up escalation after 5 days silence**
> 👤 "It's been 5 days with no response. Draft a follow-up escalating to the general manager."
> 🤖 Follow-up email with escalating tone, reference to the original claim date, and a CC suggestion for the corporate guest-relations address.

**3. 在商场丢了 AirPods 怎么找回**
> 👤 "在上海环球港逛街，回家发现包包里的AirPods丢了。不知道是被人偷了还是掉在哪了。怎么找回来？"
> 🤖 商场丢失找回流程：打开苹果"查找"App定位截图→打商场服务台调监控（大型商场保留7天）→找楼层前台填遗失登记表→联系辖区派出所（如被盗需立案）→在"上海失物招领"小程序同步登记。效率顺序：Apple查找定位→商场服务台→派出所。
